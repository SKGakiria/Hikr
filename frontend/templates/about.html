About Us

Exploring nature is great, but it is better and fun when done together. Having identified that there is a need to build a community of people with shared interests and safety when it comes to outdoor activities like hiking, we decided to build Hikr. Hikr is here to connect hikers to fellow hikers so that they can plan, link up, and explore nature as one hiking tribe. Moreover, it is safer to venture outdoors together. Hikr makes it easy to connect, socialize and keep fit while in the safety of a communit.
This project was built in two weeks by Evance Odoyo and Stacy Gakiria, software engineering students at ALX Software Engineering program. It is our submission as the end of foundations Portfolio Project.
